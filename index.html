Multi-Power Distribution MUX DC Motor Controller

A custom-built control system that operates multiple DC and AC motors using just one controller and a compact switching logic. This system intelligently distributes power based on sensor inputs, enabling full control over motion along X, Y, Z, and rotational axes — while using only two control lines. Includes integrated PID voltage feedback, safety cutoffs, and PLC compatibility. Designed for scalable, sensor-based automation with minimal hardware overhead.

Frequency Based Microfluidic Low Viscosity Filter

An experimental filtration system that controls fluid flow using vibration frequency instead of pressure or mechanical valves. A piezoelectric pump sends wave pulses through a microchannel, and sensors detect whether the signal has stabilized at a desired frequency; only then is fluid allowed to pass. This allows precise, non-mechanical filtering — ideal for lab-on-a-chip or biomedical applications requiring compact and reliable fluid handling.
